# Oh Craps! for iOS

An accessible iOS app that brings together my full library of Craps strategies from the [Oh Craps! Strategies site](https://marconius.com/craps/).

## App Features

* Searchable and filterable list of core Craps strategies.
* Alphabetized strategy sections, plus a Favorites section pinned to the top when favorites exist.
* Filter core strategies by Buy-in amount or Table Minimum.
* Detailed strategy views with Buy-in, Table Minimum, Notes, Credits, and full step-by-step play instructions.
* Native sharing for strategies through the iOS share sheet.
* User-created strategy support with create, edit, duplicate, delete, and local save flows.
* Strategy submission flow for sending user-created strategies to be considered for future app updates.
* What's New sheet that appears after app updates and remains available from the About screen.
* Accessibility-first design with strong VoiceOver support, Dynamic Type support, and careful focus management across list, detail, and editing flows.
* Craps table-inspired theming that matches the online Oh Craps experience.

## Roadmap

* Continued performance tuning as the core strategy library grows.
* Continued accessibility and usability refinement based on real device testing.
* Ongoing additions of new strategies from the main Oh Craps project.

## Tech Stack

* SwiftUI
* MessageUI
* UserDefaults for lightweight local persistence
