<h3>66 In for Out</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $600-$1000</p>
<p>Table Minimum: Any</p>
<p>Credit: Joe Rose</p>
<ol>
<li>Wait for a Point to be established.</li>
<li>Place $88 Inside; $20 each on the 5 and 9, $24 each on the 6 and 8.</li>
<li>After the first hit, drop in $1 to add to the winnings and use it all to press your bets up to $110 Inside; $25 each on the 5 and 9, $30 each on the 6 and 8.</li>
<li>On the next hit, take down all of your Inside bets and replace them with $60 Outside; $15 each on the 4, 5, 9, and 10, and no bets on the 6 and 8. You are now playing with profit.</li>
<li>On the first hit of any Outside number, use the winnings to press both the rolled number and the sister number up 1 unit each and collect the rest. So if the 5 hits, press both the 5 and 9 up to $20 each.</li>
<li>Once you've pressed all your numbers up twice, use the next hit to place the 6 and 8.</li>
<li>Continue any collect and press system you like, and restart on a 7 Out.</li>
</ol>
