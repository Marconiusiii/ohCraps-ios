<h3>ACE (Across, Craps, Eleven)</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $400</p>
<p>Table Minimum: $5</p>
<p>Notes: Betting $34 per shooter. Double this for $10 tables.</p>
<ol>
<li>Wait for a Point to be established, then on a $5 table, bet $32 across all the Place numbers. Bet $2 on the C&E.</li>
<li>Replenish the C&E if it doesn't hit.</li>
<li>Play by ear on collecting, pressing, and regressing Place bets. Leave them Off on the Come Out roll.</li>
</ol>
