<h3>Bruce's Back Wall</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $1500</p>
<p>Table Minimum: $5-$25</p>
<p>Notes: Good for choppy tables or lots of 7s on the come out.</p>
<ol>
<li>Start with a $25 Don't Pass bet. Lay $180 across, or $30 Lay bets on all of the Box numbers. Hedge with a $2 Yo Eleven bet and a $1 Boxcars bet.</li>
<li>If a point is established, take down all of the Lay bets and lay odds for the Don't Pass bet. 
	<ul role="list">
	<li>Lay $60 in odds for a Point of 6 or 8.</li>
	<li>Lay $90 in odds for a Point of 5 or 9.</li>
	<li>Lay $120 in odds for a Point of 4 or 10.</li>
	</ul></li>
<li>Play for the 7 out.</li>
</ol>
