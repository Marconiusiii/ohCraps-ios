<h3>Double Don't</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $200</p>
<p>Table Minimum: $5</p>
<p>Notes: Only betting up to $20 per shooter.</p>
<ol>
<li>Start with a $5 Don't Pass bet.</li>
<li>Once a Point is established, Lay $10 in Odds for the $5 Don't Pass bet. Make a $5 Don't Come bet.</li>
<li>If Don't Come number is set, remove Lay odds and ride out the rest of the roll waiting for a 7 Out.</li>
</ol>
