<h3>Double Tap &amp; Shave</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $600-$1000</p>
<p>Table Minimum: Any</p>
<p>Notes: Works and scales on all table minimums, just always start one unit above the minimum to give room for regression. Set bankroll appropriately for 10 shooters.</p>
<ol>
<li>Wait for a Point to be established.</li>
<li>Place bets across all of the box numbers set one unit above the table minimum. For a $10 table, place $96 across; $15 each on the 4, 5, 9, and 10, and $18 each on the 6 and 8. Scale this up or down based on the table minimum.</li>
<li>When any place bet hits, use the double-tap power press method. Instead of a full press, add more money in from your rack to power press the number up to a higher level. If the 6 or 8 hit, drop in $3 to press the bet up to $42. If the 5 or 9 hit, drop in $4 to press the bet up to $40. Treat the 4 and 10 like one number, pressing them both simultaneously if they hit. Drop in $1 to press them both up to $30 each.</li>
<li>Power press each place bet if they haven't been pressed yet.</li>
<li>as soon as any power pressed bet hits, immediately "shave" one unit off of all the place bets, so take $5 off of every bet on the 4, 5, 9, and 10 and $6 off of the 6 and 8 bets, regardless if they've been power pressed or not.</li>
<li>From here, start any collect and press system that you like. Be sure to tip your dealer due to all the place bet manipulation!</li>
</ol>
