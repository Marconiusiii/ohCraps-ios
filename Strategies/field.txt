<h3>Field Martingale</h3>
<p>Buy-in: Any</p>
<p>Table Minimum: Any</p>
<ol>
<li>At any phase of the game, bet table minimum on the Field.</li>
<li>If Field bet wins, parlay the first win.</li>
<li>On second hit, press the bet 50% and rack the rest.</li>
</ol>
