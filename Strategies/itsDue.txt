<h3>It's Due</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $600</p>
<p>Table Minimum: $5-$10</p>
<p>Notes: System that runs on the expectation that the 6 and 8 are always Due to roll based on the dice combinations. Strategy is scalable up and down for any bankroll or table minimum, but the following is for a $10 table.</p>
<ol>
<li>Wait for a Point to be established.</li>
<li>Place $64 Across; $10 each on the 4, 5, 9, and 10, and $12 each on the 6 and 8.</li>
<li>If the 6 or 8 hit, power press whichever bet hit up to $30.</li>
<li>If any of the other box numbers hit, take down the 4 and 10 bets and use those plus the winnings to press the 6 and 8 up to $30 each.</li>
<li>At this point, if the 5 or 9 hit, take down the 5 and 9 bets and drop in $2 from your rack to press the 6 and 8 up $18 each again, bringing them up to $48 each.</li>
<li>At this stage, let your rack money management dictate what to do. If you are up and in profit, use the next hit on the 6 or 8 to spread back out to the other place numbers. If you are down, take either the 6 or 8 down to $30, or regress back down to $64 Across, or stay on the Inside until you can pull in more profit.</li>
<li>Start any power press and collect system that you like from here on out.</li>
</ol>
