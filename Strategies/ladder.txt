<h3>Ladder</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $500</p>
<p>Table Minimum: $5</p>
<ol>
<li>Bet $30 on Don't Pass Line.</li>
<li>When Point is established, bet $30 on Don't Come. Replenish this bet if 11 rolls and keep betting on the DC until 4 DC points are established.</li>
<li>Once Don't Come bet moves to a number, immediately Place the number for $30. If the number hits, take down the Place bet and continue betting on DC.</li>
</ol>
