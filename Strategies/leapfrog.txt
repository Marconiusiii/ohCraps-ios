<h3>Leapfrog</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $350-$400</p>
<p>Table Minimum: $5-$10</p>
<p>Notes: Get profit within 3 hits of the place numbers with a minimal investment.</p>
<ol>
<li>Wait for a point to be established.</li>
<li>Place the 6 and 8 for $18 each for a starting outlay of $36.</li>
<li>When either the 6 or 8 hit, you'll win $21. Drop in $9 and use that plus the winnings to place the 5 and 9 for $15 each. You are now in a total of $45.</li>
<li>On any hit of the inside numbers, you'll win $21. Use the winnings to place the 4 and 10 for $10 each and collect the $1 change.</li>
<li>If any of the box numbers hit, take all the place bets down but press the 6 and 8 up to $24 each. You are  fully out of the hand and can start any spreading and progressive betting method you choose.</li> 
</ol>
