<h3>The Oreo</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $500-$1000</p>
<p>Table Minimum: $5-$10</p>
<p>Notes: A Dark side strategy stuffed with Light side action in the middle to get the best of both worlds.</p>
<ol>
<li>Start with a $10 Don't Pass bet.</li>
<li>Depending on which number becomes the Point, you'll Lay enough money against the number to win back $20:
	<ul>
	<li>If the 4 or 10 is the Point, Lay $40 in Don't Pass odds.</li>
	<li>If 5 or 9 become the Point, Lay $30 in Don't Pass Odds.</li>
	<li>If 6 or 8 become the Point, Lay $24 in Don't Pass Odds.</li>
	</ul></li>
<li>If the 6 or 8 is the Point, place the sister number for $12. If any other number is the Point, place both the 6 and 8 for $12 each. Make a $10 Come bet.</li>
<li>Continue to make $10 Come bets throughout the hand until a 7 out.</li>
<li>If the 6 or 8 have a Place bet and they roll and receive a Come bet, take down the Place bet.</li>
<li>In a situation where the Point is hit and you have at least 3 other Come bets on the table, make a $10 Any 7 bet on the Come Out roll to hedge yourself against the 7. You can also Hop the 7s instead.</li>
</ol>
