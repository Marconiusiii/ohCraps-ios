<h3>Power Craps</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $560</p>
<p>Table Minimum: $10</p>
<ol>
<li>Start with 1 unit on the Pass Line.</li>
<li>Once Point is established, Place the 6 and 8 for $18 each. Set down 1x in Pass Line Odds.</li>
<li>If the 6 or 8 hit, press bet up 50%.</li>
<li>If Point hits, the next time a Point is established, increase Pass Line Odds by 50%.</li>
</ol>
