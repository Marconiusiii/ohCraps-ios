<h3>Shinsho Strategy</h3> 
<p>Buy-in: $600</p>
<p>Table Minimum: $15</p>
<ol>
<li>Play $5 on the Fire Bet, table minimum on Pass Line if shooting or if required to play bonus bets.</li>
<li>When Point is established, place the 6 and 8 for $30 each. Bet $15 on the Field, and hop the 5s for $1 each.</li>
<li>If Field bet hits, move bet and winnings up to Place the 5 and 9; stop playing the Field bet after doing this. If Field bet loses, repeat the $15 bet and Hop the 5s.</li>
<li>Start collecting and pressing with each hit on the inside numbers. Collect the first hit, then do a full press on the second hit, and so on.</li>
</ol>
