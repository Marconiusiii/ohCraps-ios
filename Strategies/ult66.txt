<h3>Ultimate 66</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $700</p>
<p>Table Minimum: $5-$15</p>
<p>Notes: $69 per shooter.</p>
<ol>
<li>Wait for a Point to be established or set bets up in the Coming Out roll. Bet $66 across the inside numbers and have them always working.</li>
<li>If 6 or 8 hits, drop the dealer $3 and press them both up to $30 each.</li>
<li>If 5 or 9 hit, use the winnings to press them both up to $25 each.</li>
<li>Let bets ride until a 7 Out.</li>
</ol>
