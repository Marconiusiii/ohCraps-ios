<h3>Work the Come</h3>
<p>Buy-in: $500</p>
<p>Table Minimum: $5</p>
<ol>
<li>Start with minimum Pass Line bet.</li>
<li>When Point is established, place 2x Pass Line Odds. Make a 1 unit/$5 Come Bet, and bet 2 units across all the Place Number excluding the Point.</li>
<li>When a Place number hits, take it down and use the winnings to put 2x Odds on the resulting Come Bet moving to the number.</li>
<li>Keep making Come Bets and using the Place Bet winnings for the odds. Maintain this flow even after all Place Bets have been replaced by Come Bets.</li>
<li>Additional Plays:
<ul role="list">
<li>Place $1 each on all the Hard Ways. When Hard Way number hits, move the bet plus the winnings to the Field and try to double your money.</li>
<li>Whenever you feel like it, take all winnings from a roll and dump them into the Field to go for a big win. This goes for Horn, Hard Ways, and Come Bets.</li>
</ul></li>
</ol>
